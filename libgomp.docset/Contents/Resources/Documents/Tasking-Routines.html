3.4 Tasking Routines ¶

Routines relating to explicit tasks. They have C linkage and do not throw exceptions.

  • omp_get_max_task_priority – Maximum priority value
  • omp_in_explicit_task – Whether a given task is an explicit task
  • omp_in_final – Whether in final or included task region
